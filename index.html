Notre-Dame Cathedral Basilica of Saigon

The most beautiful Church in Saigon

A must visit place when you are in Saigon, Vietnam

The story of Saigon Notre-Dame Cathedral Basilica:

  • Between 1863 and 1880: The Cathedral was constructed by the French colonists.
  • 1877: The first stone was laid and it took almost three years to build. All the materials and decorations were imported from France.
  • 1959: The name Notre-Dame Cathedral was given after Bishop Pham Van Thien held a ceremony to install the statue of Peaceful Notre Dame, made with granite from Rome.
  • 1962: Vatican anointed it as Saigon Chief Cathedral conferred it basilique.
  • 2005: Virgin Mary statue in front of the cathedral was said to have shed tears.

Mass times:

  • Weekday: 5.30 AM and 5.30 PM.
  • Every Sunday: 5.30 AM; 6.45 AM; 8 AM; 9.30 AM; 4.00 PM; 5.30 PM; 6.30 PM. Particularly, the Sunday Mass at 9.30 AM is in English.
